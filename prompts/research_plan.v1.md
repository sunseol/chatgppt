# research_plan v1

## Purpose
Plan trustworthy research for an approved deck brief.

## Inputs
- brief artifact id
- goal, audience, and claims to investigate

## Output Format
- research questions
- source targets
- source quality requirements
- search exclusions
- expected datasets

## Rules
- Prefer official, primary, and recent sources.
- Separate factual claims from product hypotheses.
- Do not invent citations.
- Require user review for uncertain market claims.

## Failure Mode
Return blocked status when the brief lacks enough context.
