# PRD: DF-154 Manual QA Scenarios

## Goal

DeckForge has a manual QA scenario guide that a QA tester can follow to validate a new-user 10-minute project creation flow, 5-minute editing flow, and final report comprehension.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The document has a step-by-step checklist.
- The document names observation metrics.
- The document defines pass/fail thresholds.
- The document includes a QA dry-run record template.

## Non-goals

- Do not add browser automation for this ticket.
- Do not require live provider credentials.
- Do not replace the automated happy-path E2E test.
